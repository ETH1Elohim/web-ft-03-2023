Common Trading Signals

Understanding common ways that markets move is important to your success as an investor or trader.
These are some of the most common trading signals in markets today.

Head & Shoulders

Inverse and regular head & shoulders signals are some of the strongest signals in markets.
Inverse H&S is a bullish indicator and a regular H&S is a bearish indicator.

Double Top/Bottom

A double top is a common "market top" signal. A double bottom is a common "market bottom" signal.

Triple Top/Bottom

Triple tops and bottoms are less common signals but can often be indicating a market reversal is due.

Ascending/Descending Triangle

Ascending and descending triangle are a common trading signal and can often indicate a redirection of markets.

Symmetrical Triangle

Sometimes called "flags" symmetrical triangle indicate "consolidation" of markets and mean a breakout either up or down could be coming soon.
